# Video Standard

Use this style standard before building scenes. The goal is to make every video feel consistent without repeating design instructions each time.

## Visual Style

- Overall style: clean GitHub-style technical tutorial.
- Audience: beginner developers and AI builders.
- Mood: clear, calm, practical, not flashy.
- Layout: simple diagrams, large readable text, strong spacing.

## Colors

- Background: warm off-white, `#F7F4EF`.
- Main text: near-black, `#101418`.
- Muted text: gray, `#5B6570`.
- Primary accent: OpenAI-like green, `#10A37F`.
- Secondary accent: clear blue, `#36C5F0`.
- Warning or contrast accent: soft red, `#F65A5B`.

## Typography

- Use a clean modern sans-serif font.
- Keep titles large and short.
- Keep body copy readable from a phone screen.
- Do not put long paragraphs on screen.

## Cards

- Use floating cards only when they help separate concepts.
- Card radius: 8px.
- Card background: light translucent or subtle gradient.
- Shadow: soft and low contrast.
- Avoid nested cards.

## Background

- Keep the layout transparent-friendly.
- Avoid noisy decoration.
- Avoid random blobs or purely decorative gradients.

## Motion

- Use calm transitions.
- Prefer fade, slide, draw-line, and scale-in.
- Avoid excessive animation.
- One scene should teach one idea.
